Our Waitlist Process

  1. Contact us with your preferences (coat, color, gender, timeline).
  2. Submit a $200 deposit to secure your place in line for a specific litter.
  3. We’ll notify you the day the litter is born with photos and individual pup info.
  4. Choose your pup by waitlist order. First deposit = first pick.
  5. Remaining balance is due at pickup when pups are 8 weeks old.
